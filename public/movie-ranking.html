The Best Movies According to Edward Salonik

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The bee keeper

TI enjoy this movie for its intense action and the unique concept of a vigilante beekeeper taking on a corrupt organization with precision and purpose.

Prison break

I love the brilliant storytelling and the genius-level planning of Michael Scofield, which keeps me on edge with every twist and escape strategy.

The hard target

The gripping action and relentless pursuit of justice make this film exciting, with a strong protagonist who never backs down from a fight.
